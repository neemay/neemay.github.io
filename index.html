Yarden Ne'eman

I am a graduate student currently attending Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI) in Troy, New York. In May 2018 I received my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Information Technology and Web Science from RPI, Summa Cum Laude. I expect to receive my Master of Science in Computer Science in May 2019. For my graduate research, I am a member of the Tetherless World Constellation under the advisorship of Deborah L. McGuinness. My interests include software development, web development, data science, and semantic web. I am currently the Vice President of the RPI chapter of Upsilon Pi Epsilon, the computer science honor society. I am also the current Vice President of the RPI founding chapter of Gamma Nu Eta, the information technology honor society. My hobbies outside of computer science include theatre, knitting, and indoor rock climbing.
